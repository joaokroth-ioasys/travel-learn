# City Life — Japan Style

Japanese counterpart to the German diorama in `map-2.png`. Same rendering
style, Japanese architecture and city fabric. This file is both the visual
style guide and the image-generation prompt.

## Shared style (keep identical to the German map)

- **3D isometric miniature / tilt-shift diorama**, viewed from a high angle,
  vertical (portrait) frame.
- **Warm dusk light**, long soft shadows, windows just starting to glow.
- Muted, slightly desaturated palette; toy-model / architectural-maquette feel.
- A **river** crossing the scene with bridges (the German map's spine element).
- Clear **districts** so each structure reads as its own place, connected by
  streets and rail.

## What changes for Japan

Swap European stone/half-timbered blocks and the cathedral for Japanese urban
fabric: dense low-rise blocks with flat roofs and rooftop water tanks, narrow
streets, neon/signboard shopping streets, elevated rail viaducts, and a
traditional temple/shrine district instead of a cathedral. The river gets a
cherry-tree-lined promenade instead of a formal European park.

## The 7 structures (Japanese counterparts)

| # | Structure | Japanese rendering |
|---|-----------|--------------------|
| 1 | **Airport** | Modern airport on the edge — long runway, sleek terminal with a wave-form roof (Kansai/Haneda feel), planes at gates. |
| 2 | **Train station** | The centerpiece. Large station with **elevated rail viaducts (高架)** fanning out, a Shinkansen platform, a busy plaza (駅前) below. Replaces the German glass-roof Bahnhof. |
| 3 | **Restaurant** | A neon **shopping/eating street (商店街)** — red lanterns, vertical kanji signs, izakaya and ramen shops packed along a narrow lane. Replaces the German café row. |
| 4 | **Hospital** | Clean white/pale-blue modern hospital block with a green cross and a small ambulance bay. |
| 5 | **Tourist guide** | The landmark district: a **traditional temple/shrine** with a pagoda, a red **torii gate**, and a stone-lantern path — this is the "sightseeing" anchor (replaces the cathedral). |
| 6 | **Gym / park** | A **Japanese garden park** by the river: cherry trees (sakura), a curved red bridge over a koi pond, raked gravel. Optional small gym building at its edge. Replaces the formal fountain park. |
| 7 | **Public services** | A civic block — **city hall (市役所)**, a mid-rise government building with flags and a tidy forecourt. |

## Layout suggestion (mirror the German composition)

- **Top edge:** airport + runway.
- **Upper-middle:** train station with viaducts, neon eating street beside it.
- **River across the middle**, bridges connecting districts.
- **Center:** temple/shrine landmark (where the cathedral sat).
- **Lower-left:** Japanese garden park with the red bridge and koi pond.
- **Lower-right:** modern towers + city hall (public services) + hospital.
